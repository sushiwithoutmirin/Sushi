Mirin-Free Sushi Restaurants

Finding alcohol-free sushi can be difficult because Mirin is a hidden ingredient in most sushi rice. This platform identifies and verifies sushi restaurants that offer Mirin-free sushi rice, providing a safe and reliable resource for those with specific religious or dietary needs since many people have religious, dietary, or personal reasons for avoiding alcohol in food, yet this information is rarely disclosed by restaurants.

Have any comments, suggestions, or information about a new sushi restaurant that isn't on our list? Please email us at: admin@mirinfreesushi.com
